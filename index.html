About Us

Caysi's Corner is an online store dedicated to providing essentials to those in Elementary, Middle, and Highschool. Caysi's Corner was founded in 2016 by Caysi Lewis after she dreamed of providing kids with things they will actually use in school. Today, Caysi's Corner ships to all 50 states and 12 countries. We pride ourselves in delivering quality product for an inexpensive price, and getting you your product in a reasonable amount of time. Please enjoy all that we have to offer!
